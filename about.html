Behind the Lens

Hey! My name is Michael Tommasone and I love anything that involves a lens! I am 22 years old and I was born, died, and brought back in Mississauga, Ontario, Canada.

Those who know me would say I'm always smiling and put others before myself. I always bring my camera with me no matter where I go and I enjoy capturing the little moments people tend to forget. I enjoy people reliving their memories through my photos, and always try to tell a story in every image.

My Work

I am very into shooting for bands during their concerts. Every concert I would find new music, new friends, and new bands who wanted photos. Some photos of mine even ended up on their spotify page!
But then COVID.
Live concerts took a spill, the world took a spill, everyone has been affected one way or another, so we all try to stay safe.
Since the outbreak of COVID I have been trying to spread love and happiness to others. Whether sharing photos to people through my instagram page or asking strangers if they want photos of themselves/friends, in the end, every photo is for the enjoyment of others.
